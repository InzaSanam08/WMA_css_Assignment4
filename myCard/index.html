🐭🐱 Tom and Jerry – Ek Anokhi Dosti Ki Daud Bhari Kahani "Tom and Jerry" sirf ek cartoon nahi, ek ehsaas hai. Ek billi (Tom) aur ek choohe (Jerry) ki woh kabhi-na-khatm hone wali ladai, jo asal mein ek dosti ka dusra naam hai. Har chase, har trap, aur har punch ke peeche chhupi hoti hai ek pyari si cheekh-chillahat wali mohabbat. Tom har waqt Jerry ko pakadne ke mission pe hota hai, lekin har baar Jerry apni shatir chaalaki se usse nikal jaata hai — aur hum har baar has has ke lot-pot ho jaate hain. Kabhi frying pan ka vaar, kabhi mousetrap ki chaal, aur kabhi dynamite ka dhamaka — yeh dono kabhi rukte nahi! Par jo sabse khoobsurat baat hai, woh yeh ke jab bhi kisi ek par bura waqt aata hai... doosra sab kuch bhool kar uski madad karta hai. Shayad isi liye hum kehte hain: "Dushmani mein bhi agar dosti jhalak jaaye, to samajh lo Tom aur Jerry waale emotions hain!" Yeh cartoon bachpan ka woh hissa hai jahan har musibat mein mazaak chhupa hota tha, aur har mazaak mein ek chhoti si yaari ❤️
